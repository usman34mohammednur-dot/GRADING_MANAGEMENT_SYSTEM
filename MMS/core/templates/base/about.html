Mark Management System

Welcome to Mark Management System (MMS) — a user‑friendly platform built to simplify student academic records management.

Developed by Usman Mohammednur, MMS offers a clear interface for teachers to submit student marks and for students to retrieve their results using name, code, and grade.

Designed for Ifa Boru Special Boarding School Grades 9–12, supporting both Natural and Social streams, MMS ensures accurate academic tracking for all students.

Key Features:

  • Teacher Submission Page: Secure and structured input of student data and marks.
  • Student Portal: Instant access to individual academic performance.
  • Clean, responsive design: Works well on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
